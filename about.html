About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and dedication to providing the best grocery shopping experience.

Welcome to Penafort Supermarket

Penafort Supermarket is a subsidiary of Penafort Energy Nigeria Limited, dedicated to providing high-quality groceries, household items, and fast-moving consumer goods to the community of Iba, Ojo, Lagos and beyond.

Established with a vision to transform the retail experience in our community, we've grown to become a trusted name in the grocery retail sector. Our commitment to quality, convenience, and exceptional customer service has made us the preferred choice for many families.

At Penafort Supermarket, we believe that shopping for essentials should be a pleasant experience. That's why we've created a modern, clean, and well-organized shopping environment where you can find everything you need under one roof.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading supermarket chain in Lagos, known for our extensive product range, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service. We aim to revolutionize the grocery shopping experience through innovation and customer-centric approaches.

We envision a future where every household in our community has access to quality products that enhance their quality of life, all delivered with the warmth and care that defines Penafort Supermarket.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with a wide variety of high-quality products at competitive prices in a clean, comfortable, and friendly shopping environment.

We are committed to:

  • Sourcing the freshest and highest quality products
  • Maintaining fair and competitive pricing
  • Delivering exceptional customer service
  • Supporting local producers and suppliers
  • Being an active and responsible member of our community

Years Experience

Our Journey

  • 2010

    Founding of Penafort Energy Nigeria Limited

    Penafort Energy Nigeria Limited was established as a dynamic company with diverse business interests.

  • 2012

    Launch of Penafort Supermarket

    Penafort Supermarket opened its doors in Iba, Ojo, Lagos, with a mission to provide quality groceries and exceptional service.

  • 2015

    Expansion of Product Range

    We expanded our product range to include a wider variety of groceries, household items, and fast-moving consumer goods.

  • 2018

    Recognition as a Leading Retailer

    Penafort Supermarket was recognized as one of the leading grocery retailers in the area, known for quality products and service.

  • 2023

    Launch of Online Shopping Platform

    We embraced the digital age by launching our online shopping platform, making it more convenient for customers to shop.

  • 2025

    Future Plans

    Looking ahead, we aim to expand our reach, enhance our service offerings, and continue to be the preferred supermarket for our community.
